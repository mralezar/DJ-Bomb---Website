Biography

Vincent Pech, professionally known as DJ Bomb, is a rising talent in the DJ world, bringing energy, versatility, and a deep-rooted passion for music to every stage he steps on.

Born into a family of DJs — his mother, DJ Geenee, a graduate of the Beat Junkie Institute of Sound, and his father, DJ Hydroe, a seasoned professional with over 25 years of experience — DJ Bomb was destined to follow in their footsteps.

With over three years of hands-on experience, DJ Bomb has quickly made a name for himself in the Greater Los Angeles music scene. His resume includes an impressive range of events — bars, clubs, weddings, proms, and corporate events. His ability to read a crowd and effortlessly transition across varying genres has made him a standout up-and-comer.

A technically skilled artist, DJ Bomb is fluent on a variety of DJ setups and equipment, and is known for his adaptability, professionalism, and performance-ready mindset. Currently, he works with Power 106, one of LA's top hip-hop radio stations, while also building a growing presence across social media platforms.

Whether it's a high-energy club set or a private event, DJ Bomb brings the heat — carrying on the legacy of his family while forging his own path in the next generation of DJ culture.
